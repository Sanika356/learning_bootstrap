Dagdusheth Ganpati temple

The Dagadusheth Halwai Ganapati temple is a Hindu Temple located in Pune and is dedicated to the Hindu god Ganesh. The temple is visited by over one hundred thousand pilgrims every year.

Friendship Garden

The Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden is a Japanese-style garden in Pune, India that symbolizes the friendship between Pune and Okayama, Japan. It's the biggest Japanese garden in Asia outside of Japan.

SwamiNarayan Temple
